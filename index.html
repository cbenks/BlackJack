BLACK JACK

Instructions

  • The goal of BlackJack is to have the total value of your cards equal to 21, or as close as possible without going over!
  • A is equal to 1 or 11. K, Q, and J are equal to 10, and the rest of the cards are equal to their respective values.
  • You will always go first. You begin with two cards face up, while you can only see one of the dealers two cards to begin.
  • Now you will decide whether to "HIT" - take another card OR "STAND" - end your turn and stay with the two cards you have.
  • If your cards total value is over 21 you automatically lose. If it is 21, you automatically win. Otherwise it will be the dealers turn to go.
  • After the dealer goes, whoevers final hand is closer to 21 wins, and if it is a tie then it is considered a "PUSH" and there is no winner.
